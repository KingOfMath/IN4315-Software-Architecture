### Case Studies

- Santander

  Customers who were not doing international transfers are now using the service, customers who were using international transfers are now doing it more, and customers who had gone to use fintech competition have come back because of the One Pay offering.

- NIUM

  Through Nium's use of Ripple in the Philippines and Mexico corridors, they have been able to eliminate pre-funding requirements and offer faster remittances at a lower cost.

- SBI Remit

  It is their duty to continuously search for superior technological solutions to deliver ever improved remittance flows. Ripple helps open up new revenue potential for business and a better overall experience for customers.

# Required Capabilities

- Enable financial institutions who are part of the network-RippleNet-to send money globally, instantly, reliably, and for fractions of a penny.
- Solve three key issues with payments
    -   Speed and certainty
    -   Liquidity management
    -   Transparency
- Provide customers quick access to new markets, expand their services and deliver the best customer experience in global payments.
- Enable RippleNet customers to leverage the digital asset XRP to bridge two currencies in three seconds, ensuring payments are quickly sent and received in local currency on either side of the transaction.

# Product Context
XRP is a public, counterparty-free asset native to the XRP Ledger. It is traded on th eopen market and made available for anyone to access. The `rippled` server written in C++ powers the XPR Ledger.
